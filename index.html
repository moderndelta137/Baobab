Features:

  • Unity 2D Fast-Paced ACT game.
  • Self-made textures.
  • Customize your own character.
  • Wield two weapons simultaneously.
  • Tons of Weapons and Parts.
  • Cool Enemy design.

Instructions:

To play the demo:

Download the .rar and unzip.

  • W-A-S-D to Move.
  • Q-E to Pick up/Throw away items with Left hand-Right hand.
  • J-K to use Left hand-Right hand items.
  • Space to Jump.
  • F to Kick.
  • G to use Attraction Beam.

To view the source code:

Download the project folder and open with Unity.(Require Unity 5.2.2 or higher)
